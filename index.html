homeostasis

You are a single voice in the vastness of 88 different notes.

While you can change your sound, you would be pretty lost alone.

But lucky for you, there are 3 friends with you!

While they are fairly lazy and have a quite crooked, statistical notion of harmony, they loyally follow you around wherever you go!
